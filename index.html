Welcome To Our Campus

Marian Girls' High School is one of the contribution schemes of Holy Ghost Fathers' Foundation in education for Tanzania. The schoolsare established to give different colors to the world of education by delivering several concepts which maximize all potential in all schoollines utilize them as educational and learning tools for students.


Education at the school level is a complex area which is not always about the delivery of subject mater. However, in it, gathered all things that will not only be completed to be explored as students, practitioners and researchers, but also as leadership materials, pedagogy, psychology, communication and so forth. However, of all these complexeties are inevitable; schools are built on three pillars of collaboration between students, parents and school's management. When one of them is lost or does not play a maximum role, certainly will appear imbalance in the process and at the end result.
