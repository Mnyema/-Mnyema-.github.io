About Us

Our Background

Marian Girls' High School was established in 1997 by the Holy Ghost Fathers (E.A. Province) in Bagamoyo ancient town. We offer a trustworthy platform between parents, students and academic institutions to publicize their information which can be easily seen and accessed worldwide.

Educational Levels offered

Ordinary Level Subjects

Advanced Level Combination

Our Mission

The school is striving to complement the role of parents, family and community in leading the girld in their care to a great fidelity to life, a sense of justice to all and love for a neighbor that will form responsible leaders for the future.

Our Vision

The goal held out to all is the full development as individuals and as a member of the society. The school will always a take it as a guide. The school motto is FOR THE GLORY OF THE MOST HIGH which means everything is directed towards the Glory of the Most High.
